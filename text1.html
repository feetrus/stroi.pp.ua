Social Call

Dear ladies and gentlemen,

Life is full of various joys and events where alcohol can play a role. However, we urge each of you to pay special attention to responsible alcohol consumption.

Alcohol is not only a source of pleasure but also a potential danger, especially when used improperly. Remember that your health and safety, as well as the safety of those around you, depend on how you consume alcohol.

Therefore, consume alcohol responsibly and wisely. Do not abuse it, do not drive after consuming it, and do not consume alcohol if you have medical contraindications.

Let's create a safe and healthy environment for ourselves and others. Remember that responsible alcohol consumption is a sign of maturity and care for oneself and others.

Best regards,
