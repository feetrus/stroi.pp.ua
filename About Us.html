About Us

Anavim Group Distribution Company is a leading player in the alcohol distribution sector in Poland. Established with a commitment to excellence and a passion for delivering quality products, we have emerged as a trusted partner for both local and international brands seeking market penetration and growth.

Our company boasts a robust distribution network spanning the entirety of Poland, ensuring that our products reach every corner of the market efficiently and promptly. Leveraging strategic partnerships with top producers and suppliers, we offer a diverse portfolio of alcoholic beverages ranging from premium wines and spirits to craft beers and specialty liquors.
